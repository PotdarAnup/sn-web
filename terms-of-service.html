2. About Our Services

Structura Nordic AB (Org. nr. 559333-6246) is a professional engineering consultancy based in Gothenburg, Sweden, providing:

  • Façade design and engineering (curtain wall, unitized, stick systems, cladding, skylights, timber & rainscreen)
  • BIM modelling and 3D coordination (LOD 100–500, Revit, Tekla, Rhino)
  • Shop drawings, fabrication drawings and technical documentation
  • Structural calculations, wind load analysis, glass and mullion sizing, thermal analysis
